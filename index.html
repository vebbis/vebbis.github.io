I am a master's student in Computer Science at NTNU, graduating in 2026. My research focuses on uncertainty quantification for frozen self-supervised vision foundation models — why standard distance-based methods fail and what representation geometry reveals about when they can be fixed. This work is done in collaboration with DNV, with applications to structural inspection. I studied at MIT as a Fulbright Scholar and at ETH Zürich. This summer, I join Cognite's Atlas AI research team to work on foundational transformer models for industrial applications.
